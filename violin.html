Carnatic Music
What is it?

Carnatic music is the south Indian traditional and classical style of music. It is an ancient form of music which is now gaining worldwide popularity.

Carnatic Violin

Carnatic music can be sung or be played on pretty much any instrument. I decided to play it on the violin, as violin always seemed like a challenge to me.
